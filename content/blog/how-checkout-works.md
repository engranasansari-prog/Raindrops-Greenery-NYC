## Start with the menu

Browse the Raindrops Greenery menu to compare Flower, Pre-Rolls, and Edibles. Each product card includes a photo, price, category, size, potency details when available, and deal information.

## Continue to checkout

When you are ready, select an order button. Checkout confirms final product availability, delivery details, payment, and age verification.

## Confirm before placing an order

Product availability and pricing can change. Always review the final checkout screen before placing an order.
