## Flower

Flower is the classic format for customers who like aroma, strain character, and flexibility. Product cards show size, price, potency, and deal information when available.

## Pre-Rolls

Pre-Rolls are built for convenience. They are easy to compare and simple to order when you want a ready-to-use format.

## Edibles

Edibles are flavor-led and measured. Always review product details carefully and follow the instructions shown during checkout.

## Compare before ordering

Use filters on the menu page to compare products by category, price, size, potency, and deals before continuing to checkout.
