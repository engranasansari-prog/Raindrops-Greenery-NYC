## Browse with less friction

The Raindrops Greenery menu is organized around three core product types: Flower, Pre-Rolls, and Edibles. The goal is simple: help you compare products quickly before moving into checkout.

Use search and filters to narrow the menu by category, brand, profile, size, price, potency, and deals.

## Complete checkout securely

When you are ready to order, use any order button to continue to checkout. Final availability, payment, verification, and delivery details are confirmed before the order is placed.

## Delivery focus

Raindrops Greenery New York delivery focuses on Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. Delivery eligibility can vary by location and is confirmed during checkout.
