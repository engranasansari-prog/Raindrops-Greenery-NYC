The Raindrops Greenery menu is organized around three core product types: **Flower Strains**, **Pre-Rolls**, and **Edibles**. The goal is simple — help you compare products quickly before moving into checkout, without scrolling through fifty SKUs of overlapping noise.

This guide walks through every filter and feature on the menu, how to use the strain finder quiz, and the fastest way to land on a product that matches what you actually want.

## Three categories, on purpose

We don't carry vape carts, concentrates, or tinctures. That's a focus decision, not an inventory gap. The three categories we do carry — Flower Strains, Pre-Rolls, Edibles — cover the vast majority of what NYC delivery customers actually order, and keeping the menu tight lets us be picky about what makes the cut.

Every product on the menu has earned its slot. We don't list trim-based pre-rolls, low-quality edibles with under-dosed gummies, or flower we wouldn't keep in our own jars.

## The filter rail

The left side of the [menu page](/menu) has the filter rail. From top to bottom:

### Category

The first filter you'll touch. Choose:

- **All** — every product across categories, sorted by curation
- **Flower** — buds in jars or pouches (3.5g, 7g, etc.)
- **Pre-Rolls** — pre-rolled joints in single or multi-packs
- **Edibles** — gummies, chocolates, baked goods

Each category gets its own icon and accent color so you can scan quickly.

### Strain profile (Flower + Pre-Rolls)

When you're in Flower or Pre-Rolls, you can narrow by strain lean:

- **Indica** — heavier body feel, more relaxing
- **Sativa** — more cerebral, daytime-friendly
- **Hybrid** — balanced; the most common category
- **Indica-Hybrid** — leans relaxing
- **Sativa-Hybrid** — leans energizing

Strain types are a rough behavioral category, not a scientific guarantee — your body chemistry matters as much as the label. But it's still the fastest way to narrow toward what you want.

### Price range

A slider for the menu price range. Helpful when you have a budget cap or when you want to see only the [top shelf](/deals) — try setting the floor to $40 and see what shows.

### Potency (Flower only)

Minimum THC percentage. Set this if you want to filter out lower-THC flower. Most quality flower today runs 18% to 30% — the [✦ STICKY badge](/blog/what-sticky-icky-means) on our menu marks 30%+ flower and 1000mg+ edibles.

This filter is intentionally Flower-only because edibles and pre-rolls have different potency conventions (mg vs %, dose vs concentration).

### Size

Filter by pack size — 3.5g, 7g, multi-pack pre-rolls, edible package sizes. Useful when you know exactly what format you want.

### Brand

A search-as-you-type field. Type the first letters of a brand name to filter the grid to that brand's products.

### Deals only

A toggle that surfaces our curated picks — same selection logic as our [/deals page](/deals), but filtered into the main menu grid.

## How to find what you want in under a minute

A few patterns that work:

**"I want something strong."**
Set Category to Flower, drag the THC potency slider to 25% minimum, and sort by ✦ STICKY badge. You'll land in our Heavy Hitters section.

**"I want something for evening."**
Set Strain profile to Indica or Indica-Hybrid. Browse by description — most evening-friendly strains call this out directly.

**"I want a quick, discreet edible."**
Set Category to Edibles. Look for 5–10mg per-piece doses if you're new; 25mg+ if you're experienced. Don't double up before 90 minutes have passed.

**"I want a pre-roll for one session."**
Set Category to Pre-Rolls. Single 0.5g or 1g cones are the standard "one session" unit. Multi-packs are better value if you'll use them over a few days.

**"I have no idea what to get."**
Take the [strain finder quiz](/quiz). Answer 5 questions about how you want to feel and we'll match three products from the current menu.

## The product detail dialog

Tap any product card to open the detail dialog. You'll see:

- A bigger product photo
- The full brand description
- Size variants (if there are multiple sizes of the same strain — e.g., 3.5g and 7g)
- Strain profile and dominant terpenes when documented
- An "Order this" button to continue to secure checkout

The dialog closes with the X in the corner or by tapping the background. The URL updates with `?product=...` so you can share or bookmark a specific product.

## Reading potency labels

A few notes on what the numbers mean:

- **THC%** (Flower + Pre-Rolls) — percentage of total THC by dry weight. Higher = stronger. Most quality flower today is 18% to 30%.
- **THC mg** (Edibles) — total milligrams of THC per piece or per package. A 5mg gummy is a starter dose; 25mg is for experienced users; 50mg+ is for high tolerance only.
- **CBD%** or **CBD mg** — when listed, this is the CBD content. CBD moderates the THC experience for some users — useful in edibles, mostly negligible in standard flower.

If a product doesn't list CBD, assume it's a THC-dominant product with minimal CBD.

## When to use the strain finder quiz

If you're a new customer, the [quiz](/quiz) is the fastest path to a confident order. It asks:

1. How you want to feel (energized, balanced, relaxed)
2. What time of day you'll consume
3. What format you prefer (Flower / Pre-Rolls / Edibles)
4. How experienced you are
5. Budget range

In 60 seconds you'll see three matched products from current inventory. It's not a substitute for browsing — it's a substitute for analysis paralysis.

## Pricing — what you'll see vs. what you'll pay

The price on the card is the price at checkout. No New York State cannabis excise tax, no state sales tax, no delivery fee on orders over $25. [Here's the full explanation.](/blog/why-tax-free-shinnecock)

The math:

- Menu price $80 = checkout total $80 = handoff total $80
- Add a 10mg edible pack at $20 → menu price $100, checkout $100, handoff $100, plus your free pre-roll if it's your first order

No surge pricing. No service fee. No "convenience" fee. The price doesn't change based on time of day or demand.

## Common questions

**Can I save a product for later without ordering?**
Each product has a shareable URL (the dialog updates the URL when open). Copy the URL to bookmark — that's the lightest-weight way to "save" a product.

**Do you ever have sale prices on the menu?**
Current pricing is the **final** pricing. We don't run discount codes or surprise sales — the model is "every menu price is already the lowest reasonable price for the product." We do curate selections in [/deals](/deals) by quality and value (Heavy Hitters, Top Shelf, Under $25), but those aren't discounted prices, they're curated picks.

**How often does the menu update?**
Daily as inventory rotates. Sold-out items disappear and new arrivals show up automatically — the menu reflects what we can actually deliver tonight.

**Is the menu available on mobile?**
Yes. The menu is built mobile-first; every filter works on small screens. The filter rail collapses into a drawer on mobile so the product grid uses the full screen width.
